How to use it

  • Apply a generous amount of Pure Polish to the brush.
  • Wipe any extra back inside the neck of the bottle.
  • Starting at the center of the nail, gently move the brush from the cuticle to the free edge without a lot of pressure.
  • Moving quickly, repeat on the left and the right side of the nail.
  • Apply two coats to create an even application.
  • Allow your freshly painted nails to set for at least one minute.
